## 📈 How I Work
- Design performance tests that reflect **real-world traffic patterns** — 
  not just synthetic load.
- Use metrics (response time, throughput, error rate, SLA compliance) 
  to drive **go/no-go decisions**.
- Believe quality should be **built in from day one** — not tested at 
  the end.
- Focus on **test reliability and maintainability** — flaky tests and 
  unvalidated scripts slow teams down.
- Mentor junior engineers and share knowledge through documentation 
  and team sessions.
